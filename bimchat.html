Key Features

AI-Powered Conversational BIM Intelligence

Natural Language Interaction

Ask questions in plain English and receive instant, context-aware answers—no deep BIM expertise required.

  • Intuitive Queries: "Show me all fire-rated walls on Level 3" or "What's the completion percentage of MEP works?"
  • Instant Responses: Get immediate answers without navigating complex GUIs or understanding technical jargon.

Deep BIM Intelligence

Parses model geometry, properties, and metadata, synthesizing them into concise responses and automated reports.

  • Comprehensive Analysis: Understands 3D geometry, element relationships, and hierarchical structures.
  • Visual Insights: Generates visual call-outs, highlights, and color-coded elements directly in the model.
group

Democratized Data Access

Every stakeholder can explore, verify, and act on BIM data—from architects to facility managers.

  • Universal Access: Site engineers, quantity surveyors, planners, and QA/QC teams all benefit.
  • No Training Required: Intuitive interface eliminates the steep learning curve of traditional BIM tools.
insights

Actionable Insights

Generate clash summaries, quantity take-offs, compliance checks, and more through conversational workflows.

  • Automated Reports: Instant quantity summaries, BOQ breakdowns, and progress tracking.
  • Smart Coordination: Identify missing data, flag non-conforming elements, and accelerate decision-making.
calculate

Intelligent Quantity Take-Offs

Extract normalized quantities per BOQ group with automated unit conversions and clean subtotals.

  • Flexible Reporting: Get detailed item-level lists or normalized summaries based on your needs.
  • BOQ Integration: Seamlessly organize quantities by Bill of Quantities codes and categories.
verified

Quality Control & Validation

Ensure model integrity and metadata completeness with intelligent validation and gap detection.

  • Missing Data Detection: Identify elements lacking zone, level, or system information.
  • Conformity Checks: Validate that installed elements match specifications and standards.

Who Benefits from BIMChat™?

BIMChat™ serves every stakeholder across the Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) lifecycle.

engineering

Site Engineers

"Which façade panels are still in transit?" Monitor site progress, check element status, and verify installation sequencing.

Quantity Surveyors

BIMChat™ represents a paradigm shift in how stakeholders interact with Building Information Models. Your building now "speaks back," making previously siloed information transparent, searchable, and immediately useful.

Enable seamless communication across disciplines. Everyone—from site engineers to facility managers—can access the same information in a way that makes sense to them.
